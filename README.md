### [UIDAI Advance Data Analysis 2026](https://github.com/sakshi-mahajan27/UIDAI_26)
`Python` `Pandas` `Scikit-learn` `Matplotlib` `Seaborn` · *UIDAI Data Hackathon 2026 · Digital India Initiative*

Ingested and cleaned 1M+ anonymized Aadhaar enrollment records across 3 CSV chunks. Engineered age-group percentage and dependency ratio features. Computed Gini coefficients and Lorenz curves at state, district, and pincode levels; applied Herfindahl index and Shannon entropy for regional concentration analysis. K-Means clustering with silhouette scoring on child vs. adult enrollment ratios; log-scale z-score outlier detection to surface structural super-nodes. Dual-notebook pipeline: statistical deep-dive and a visual executive summary for stakeholder reporting.
